Let's Learn Functional Programming!

28 September 2016

Learning Functional Programming

I decided to start learning a functional language.

It's a buzzword that I keep hearing and the fact of the matter is that I don't really understand what it is. I know its rooted in lambda calculus, I know that you use a lot of functions and recursions, and I know it prevents "side effects".

But I could not tell someone exactly what it is or explain it. And so I figured that should change.

Going Forward

I decided to learn Lisp (because why not). As to what "kind" of Lisp, I am not educated enough to decide, so I will just be using Common Lisp.

I will probably be working through Project Euler problems to help myself learn. And I might even livestream the process. We'll see what happens.

Livestreaming is something I have always wanted to try, and it might keep me motivated. So, maybe I'll give it a shot.

That's all for now.
